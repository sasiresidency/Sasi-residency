Thiruchendur Murugan Temple - Sacred Arupadaiveedu

Thiruchendur Murugan Temple is one of the six sacred abodes (Arupadaiveedu) of Lord Murugan, located on the beautiful shores of the Bay of Bengal in Tamil Nadu. This ancient temple holds immense spiritual significance and attracts thousands of devotees throughout the year for darshan and spiritual blessings.

Temple Timings

4:30 AM - 9:00 PM (Daily)

Distance from Lodge

1.2 kilometers (15-minute walk)

Best Time to Visit

Early morning (4:30 AM - 8:00 AM)

Special Features:

  • One of the six sacred abodes of Lord Murugan
  • Ancient temple with rich history and architecture
  • Located on the beautiful Bay of Bengal coast
  • Famous for its spiritual atmosphere and divine energy
  • Special darshan timings during festivals

Visit Tips:

  • Dress modestly and remove footwear before entering
  • Photography is not allowed inside the temple
  • Best to visit early morning for peaceful darshan
  • Carry water and wear comfortable walking shoes
  • Respect the temple customs and traditions

Local Attractions

Explore the beautiful surroundings of Thiruchendur

Thiruchendur Beach

A serene coastal stretch adjacent to the renowned Thiruchendur Murugan Temple. This beautiful beach features golden sands and clear waters, perfect for evening walks, sunset views, and enjoying the soothing sea breeze.

1.5km from lodge Best time: Evening

Manapad Beach

Located near Thiruchendur, Manapad Beach is known for its pristine golden sands, glistening blue waters, and lush greenery. The beach offers a blend of serenity, historical charm, and spirituality with picturesque rock formations and quiet walks on the sandy shore.

15km from lodge Best time: Morning

Valliamman Cave Temple

This sacred temple is dedicated to Goddess Valli, the consort of Lord Murugan. The approach involves navigating a narrow corridor and steps leading into the cave. The temple offers a serene ambiance and spiritual experience, though the path may be challenging for older visitors.

2km from lodge 6 AM - 8 PM

Vana Thirupathi Temple

Also known as Sri Punnai Srinivasa Perumal Temple, this serene temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara, resembling the idol of Tirupati Balaji. Built with a beautiful combination of northern and southern architecture, the temple offers a peaceful darshan experience with well-maintained facilities including parking, restrooms, and a restaurant.

20km from lodge 7 AM - 8 PM

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your stay

How far is the lodge from Thiruchendur Murugan Temple?

Our lodge is located just 1.2km (15-minute walk) from the Thiruchendur Murugan Temple. You can easily walk to the temple for morning and evening darshans.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

We offer flexible stay options to suit your pilgrimage and travel plan
12-Hour Stay: This is perfect for a short visit. You can check in at any time (24/7), and your check-out will be 12 hours after your check-in time.
Example:If you check in at 6:00 PM, your check-out time will be 6:00 AM the next day.

Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability.

Do all rooms have climate control?

Yes, all our rooms feature modern air conditioning for your comfort, especially important during the hot summer months in Thiruchendur.

What is the best time to visit Thiruchendur?

The best time to visit Thiruchendur is from October to March when the weather is pleasant. However, the temple is open year-round and each season has its own charm.

Do you provide food services?

We don't have an in-house restaurant, but there are many excellent restaurants and food stalls within walking distance, including vegetarian options near the temple area.

How can I book a room?

You can book a room by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or sending us a WhatsApp message. We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak seasons.

Is the lodge suitable for families with children?

Absolutely! We have family rooms that can accommodate parents and children comfortably. Thiruchendur is a family-friendly destination with the sacred temple and beautiful beach nearby.

What are the temple timings?

The sacred Thiruchendur Murugan Temple is open from 4:30 AM to 9:00 PM daily. Special timings apply during festivals and important days.

Do you offer any special packages?

We offer special rates for longer stays (weekly/monthly) and group bookings. Contact us for customized packages based on your requirements.
